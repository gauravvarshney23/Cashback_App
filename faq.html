Q: What about returns, cancellations or exchange for my order?

A: Returns and cancellations are subject to the returns and cancellations policy of the store from which you purchased from. If you return or cancel an item, we have to reverse any cashback earned from that sale. Exchanges also reverse cashback earned. During the process of an exchange, the store cancels the old order and replaces it with a new one. When the store creates the new order, it fails to capture the referral from Yoneak Services Pvt. Ltd. and thus will not credit Yoneak Services Pvt. Ltd. with a commission. To avoid losing cashback on an exchange, we recommend cancelling your order and making a new transaction.

 EARNING YOUR CASHBACK Q: What is the process of earning cashback via Yoneak Services Pvt. Ltd.?

A: Simple, just follow the easy steps below to earn cashback: 1. Login to Yoneak Services Pvt. Ltd. website 2. Search for the retailer you want to shop on 3. Click-through to their website via the link given on our site 4. Shop and pay on the retailer's site like you normally do 5. Retailer now pays us a commission for this sale. We pass that to you as 'cashback'. Initially your cashback remains in Pending status. Once the return period of your product is over, retailer pays us in the next monthly payment cycle. Your cashback then becomes Confirmed 6. When you have earned a minimum amount as Confirmed Cashback, we can pay the money to your Indian bank account via NEFT or by cheque (admin charge of will be deducted from your Cashback amount).

Q: Want to know about Pending Earnings ?

A: Pending Earnings are those that we have tracked as valid transactions and have been acknowledged by retailers. However, since they are still within the Return or Cancellation period they cannot be paid out to you yet. Let’s take an example. You make a purchase from Jabong.com for Rs. 500. Within 1-3 days you shall receive an email from us acknowledging that you have Pending Earnings of Rs. 30 (at 6% cashback rate). Now once the cancellation/return period is over, Jabong.com shall confirm this to us and pay us in the next monthly payment cycle. We shall then move Rs. 30 to your Confirmed Earnings. However, if your plans changed and you had to cancel your order, the transaction is cancelled and the cashback is removed from your Pending Earnings.

Q: Want to know about Confirmed Earnings?

A: Confirmed Earnings are those that have been approved as valid sales from retailers. When you collect a minimum amount in Confirmed cashback we can pay the money to your Indian bank account by NEFT or by cheque (admin charge will be deducted from your cashback amount). You need a minimum amount as Confirmed cashback for each payment request.

Q: How much time is taken to credit my cashback to my Yoneak Services Pvt. Ltd. account after my purchase?

A: This varies between retailers and the type of purchase. Normally after you do the transaction you would get an email from us within 72 hours stating the cashback due to you. This is called Pending cashback. Once the return period of your products is over/travel is completed, the retailer knows that the Sale was genuine, and then pays us in the next monthly payment cycle. The status of this transaction shall then automatically move to Confimed. From the time of your purchase it could take between 1-3 months for your cashback to get Confirmed.

Q: Where do I see my cashback earnings?
